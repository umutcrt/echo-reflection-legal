Privacy Policy — Echo: Reflection

Effective date: April 21, 2026

Summary

Echo: Reflection is designed with privacy in mind. The app does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. Gameplay happens entirely on your device.

What we do not collect

  • No personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, or microphone access
  • No account creation or login

What stays on your device

Your puzzle progress, streak count, settings, and other gameplay state are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs. This data is not transmitted externally. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

Sharing feature

The app includes an optional sharing feature. When you choose to share, the app generates an image and passes it to your device’s system share sheet. You control where it is sent. The app does not receive or track shared content.

Game Center

If Game Center is enabled on your device, the app may use Apple's Game Center services to unlock achievements and sync progress across your devices. This communication occurs directly between your device and Apple.

Third-party libraries

Echo: Reflection uses open-source libraries that operate locally:

  • just_audio — plays audio files locally
  • share_plus — integrates with the system share sheet
  • path_provider, shared_preferences — local storage

Children's privacy

Echo: Reflection is suitable for all ages. The app does not collect personal data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
