# Privacy Policy — Echo: Reflection

**Effective date:** April 21, 2026

## Summary

Echo: Reflection does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

## What we don't collect

- No personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No advertising identifiers
- No crash reports from third parties
- No location data
- No contacts, photos, or microphone access
- No account creation or login required

## What stays on your device

Your puzzle progress, streak count, settings preferences, and other gameplay
state are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs.
This data never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else.
If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

## Sharing feature

Echo: Reflection includes an optional share feature. When you tap "share"
after completing a daily puzzle, the app generates an image and hands it to
your device's built-in share sheet. You choose where to send it. We do not
receive, store, or track anything about what you share or with whom.

## Game Center (if enabled)

If you have signed into Game Center on your device, Echo: Reflection may use
Apple's Game Center service to unlock achievements and sync progress across
your own devices. This communication happens directly between your device
and Apple. We do not have access to your Game Center identifier or any data
associated with it. You can disable Game Center at any time in your device's
system settings.

## Third-party services

Echo: Reflection uses the following open-source libraries, none of which
transmit data to third parties:
- just_audio — plays bundled audio files locally
- share_plus — integrates with the system share sheet
- path_provider, shared_preferences — local file and preference storage

## Children's privacy

Echo: Reflection is suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect any data,
we do not collect data from children either. The app complies with COPPA and
GDPR-K by design.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with
a new effective date. Since we do not collect data, any future changes would
describe new optional features if and when they're added.
